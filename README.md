# Privacy Policy for Wallpaper App

**Effective Date**: November 6, 2025


**Last Updated**: November 6, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Wallpaper App ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Search Queries**: When you search for wallpapers, your search terms are processed to fetch relevant images from our third-party image provider.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device identifiers, device model, or operating system information.
- **Usage Data**: We do not collect analytics or track how you use the app.

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT track your browsing history outside the app
- We do NOT collect payment information (the app is free)

## How We Use Information

The limited information we process is used solely to:
- **Fetch Wallpapers**: Search queries are sent to Pexels API to retrieve relevant wallpaper images
- **Display Content**: Images and photographer attribution are displayed within the app
- **App Functionality**: Enable core features like browsing, downloading, and setting wallpapers

## Third-Party Services

### Pexels API
We use Pexels API (https://www.pexels.com) to provide wallpaper images. When you:
- Browse the gallery
- Search for wallpapers
- View image details

Your requests are sent to Pexels servers. Please review [Pexels Privacy Policy](https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/) to understand how they handle data.

**What Pexels May Collect**:
- API requests (which images you view/search)
- IP address (automatically collected by their servers)
- Request timestamps

## Permissions Required

Our app requires the following Android permissions:

### INTERNET
- **Purpose**: Fetch wallpaper images from Pexels API
- **Required**: Yes, app cannot function without internet access

### SET_WALLPAPER
- **Purpose**: Allow you to set images as your device wallpaper
- **Required**: Only if you want to use the "Set as Wallpaper" feature

### READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- **Purpose**: Read images when setting them as wallpapers
- **Required**: Only for wallpaper setting functionality
- **When Requested**: When you attempt to set a wallpaper

### WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- **Purpose**: Save/download wallpaper images to your device gallery
- **Required**: Only if you want to download wallpapers
- **When Requested**: When you attempt to download an image

**Note**: All permissions are requested only when needed for specific features. You can deny permissions, but certain features will not be available.

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- **Downloaded Images**: When you download wallpapers, they are saved to your device's Pictures folder (Pictures/Wallpapers)
- **Temporary Files**: The app may create temporary files when processing images, which are automatically deleted after use
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store any of your data on external servers

### Data Retention
- Downloaded images remain on your device until you manually delete them
- No other data is stored persistently by the app

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Transfer data outside the necessary API calls to Pexels

The only data sharing occurs when:
- Making API requests to Pexels (search queries, image requests)
- Using the native "Share" feature (you control what apps receive the image)

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences. Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the app.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- We do not store sensitive personal information
- Temporary files are cleared after use
- No user accounts or authentication required

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Control Permissions
- Revoke app permissions at any time through Android Settings > Apps > Wallpaper App > Permissions

### Delete Data
- Clear app data: Settings > Apps > Wallpaper App > Storage > Clear Data
- Delete downloaded images manually from your gallery
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### Opt-Out of Data Processing
- Stop using the app if you do not agree with this privacy policy
- Deny permissions for features you don't want to use
